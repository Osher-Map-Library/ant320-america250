## About America 250: The Hidden Agenda

*America 250: The Hidden Agenda* was curated by the students in the spring course of 2026 ANT 320: Anthropology and the Museu, taught by Dr. David Shane Lowry, Assistant Professor of Anthropology in the Department of Geography-Anthropology. This exhibition is a response to Founding Memories, the Osher Map Library's semiquincentennial exhibition.

This web exhibition was created by Paul Fuller, Coordinator of Digital Collections and Digital Initiatives at the Osher Map Library and Smith Center for Cartographic Education at the University of Southern Maine in 2026.
